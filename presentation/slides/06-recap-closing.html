Here are the best resources to continue learning. The official Ethereum docs are comprehensive. CryptoZombies teaches Solidity through building a game. Ethernaut is a security wargame where each level teaches a real vulnerability. OpenZeppelin provides battle-tested contract libraries. Hardhat and Foundry are professional dev environments. And Remix is where we started today — perfect for quick experimentation.
